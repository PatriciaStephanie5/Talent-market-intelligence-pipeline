# GTM Talent Market Intelligence Pipeline – HireFlow

**Q1 2026**  
**Automated salary benchmarking + candidate prioritization pipeline for high-volume GTM hiring**

Built for HireFlow (B2B SaaS talent intelligence platform), this end-to-end pipeline analyzes 250,000+ real job market records to deliver accurate salary benchmarks and prioritized candidate lists that enable faster, smarter GTM talent acquisition.

### STAR Story
**Situation**  
HireFlow’s recruiting team was struggling with inaccurate salary offers and low offer acceptance rates due to lack of real-time market intelligence.

**Task**  
Build a production-grade intelligence pipeline that benchmarks salaries across roles, experience levels, education, and remote status, while scoring candidates for outreach priority.

**Action**  
- Performed deep SQL analysis on 250,000+ job market records  
- Designed and implemented a weighted Candidate Priority Score (0–100) based on experience, skills count, certifications, and education level  
- Delivered clean, scored dataset ready for weekly Clay imports and Tableau visualization  

**Result**  
- Delivered accurate salary benchmarks for critical GTM roles (AI Engineer \~$173k, Product Manager \~$158k)  
- Created prioritized candidate lists that significantly improve outreach efficiency and offer acceptance rates  
- Built reusable pipeline now used for weekly talent intelligence reporting

### Key Business Insights
- Remote roles command a **+$3,562** premium on average
- Experience remains the strongest salary driver (10+ years earn \~32% more than 0–2 years)
- PhD and Master’s holders continue to command clear salary premiums
- Top-scoring candidates consistently reach 87–89 on the priority model

### Tech Stack
- SQL (SQLite) – deep market analysis & scoring
- Weighted Candidate Priority Score model
- Tableau Public – interactive salary benchmarking & prioritization dashboards
- Ready for Clay + n8n automation
