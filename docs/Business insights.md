## Executive Summary

This production pipeline analyzes 250,000+ real job market records to deliver accurate salary benchmarks and prioritized candidate lists for GTM hiring. By combining deep market intelligence with a weighted **Candidate Priority Score (0–100)**, Hireflow can now make data-driven salary offers and focus recruiting efforts on high-fit candidates.
